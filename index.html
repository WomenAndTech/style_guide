Font Sizes

Current Usage

  • xxl - Interview heading
  • xl - Pullquote
  • l - Heading
  • m - Intro paragraph text, usually following a heading.
  • s - Interview questions
  • xs - Base body font size
  • xxs - Sub-text

Size classes applied to non-heading HTML tags (e.g <p>)

Biying Miao [xxl]

Work/life balance is very difficult. [xl]

We're not endorsing the obvious. We're commending the awesome. [l]

Biying Miao is the co-founder of Hot Pop Factory. [m]

Where did you grow up and what were you like as a child? [s]

A fresh look for an important project. We’ve redesigned and relaunched Women&&Tech, let us tell you why. [xs]

Interview by RUBY WATSON [xxs]
